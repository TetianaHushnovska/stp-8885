FAQ

Is Wacky Battles free?

Yes! It's free to play with optional in-game purchases.

Do I need internet access?

Some features work offline, but an internet connection is needed for PvP.

Is it kid-friendly?

The game is suitable for teens and up, with light humor and fantasy themes.

Are updates planned?

Definitely! New characters and chapters are released regularly.

Can I play on iOS?

Currently available on Android; iOS version coming soon.
