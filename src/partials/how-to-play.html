How to Play

  • Recruit Heroes

    Unlock wacky warriors
    from fairy tale lore.

  • Form Your Strategy

    Arrange your team
    for maximum
    battle impact.

  • Engage
    in Turn-Based Combat

    Make your move
    and outwit enemies.

  • Explore
    the Enchanted Kingdom

    Unlock regions,
    complete missions,
    and discover hidden stories.

  • Climb the Ranks

    Challenge other players
    and rise through
    the PvP arena.
