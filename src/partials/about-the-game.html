About the Game

Step into Wacky Battles, a whimsical turn-based RPG set in the Enchanted Kingdom!
This midcore strategy game combines classic fairy tales, unpredictable fantasy heroes,
and deep tactical battles into one captivating adventure.

  • Build a team of unlikely heroes from magical realms

  • Make impactful decisions that influence story outcomes

  • Master tactics and abilities to overcome wild challenges

With twists at every corner, your journey through Wacky Battles will keep you hooked and laughing as you fight for glory in the land
of enchantment!
